Our Story

Founded in 2023, SDM SmartDine revolutionizes the dining experience by combining gourmet cuisine with cutting-edge technology. Our chefs blend traditional recipes with modern techniques to create unforgettable dishes.


We source 95% of our ingredients from local farmers and producers, ensuring freshness while supporting our community. Our smart dining system allows for seamless ordering and personalized menu recommendations.


With a focus on sustainability, we've eliminated single-use plastics and maintain a zero-food-waste kitchen through our innovative composting and donation programs.
